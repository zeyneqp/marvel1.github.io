The Creation of Marvel

Brief Overview of Key Events and Periods

Marvel's early years saw the creation of characters like Captain America and Namor the Sub-Mariner, popular during the Golden Age of Comics.

The 1960s ushered in a new era with the creation of iconic superheroes like Spider-Man, the Fantastic Four, and the X-Men. This period established Marvel's prominence in the comic book industry.

In the 1970s, Marvel continued to expand its universe with characters like the Black Panther, the Punisher, and storylines like the "Dark Phoenix Saga."

Marvel has maintained its status as a major player in the comic book world, introducing innovative storytelling and embracing diversity with characters like Miles Morales and Kamala Khan.

Starting in 2008 with "Iron Man," Marvel Studios launched the MCU, a highly successful film franchise that connects various superhero films and TV shows into a shared universe.
